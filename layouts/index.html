Free & Open Source

Haaukins is completely free and open source project to boost Cyber Training with unique CTF challenges. Haaukins

Fast by default

Get your environment in few secs and start to hack vulnerable devices which you can attack through Kali/Parrot over your browser.

Virtualized

Highly Automated

Setting up many machines with unique vulnerable challenges is fully automated where it can be controlled through Haaukins webclient.

No Software Requirement

Experience hacking tools, vulnerable environments and easy connection over any browser, just a button away.
